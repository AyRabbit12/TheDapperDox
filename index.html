About Us

Our names are Natalia Contreras, Rafael Mendoza and most importantly,
Astro. We started "The Dapper Dox" because we always had the most difficult time finding clothing that properly fit Astro. So we took matters into our own hands and started a clothing line just for Dachshunds! We take Astro with us everywhere so we also understood the importance of quality so we always aim to provide the best product at the best prices. Our core values are Integrity, Loyalty, Selflessness, and Empathy. We make sure we run The Dapper Dox with those same values. Not only do we want to provide a quality product that our customers can trust, but we also want to give back to our community. As we all know, Dachshunds are prone to having back problems in their lifetime which is why we started a fund to help provide surgeries to pups whose owners are having financial hardship. Pups shouldn't suffer because of a lack of money and we can all use help from time to time. To encourage our customers to donate, we even add rewards points to your account per dollar donated just like if you had bought some of our products! We look forward to doing business with you and we love hearing your feedback! So do not hesitate to reach out to let us know what you love about the Dapper Dox or what we can improve on. Stay Dapper Dudes!
